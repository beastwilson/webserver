Welcome to the world of Node Your Meme!

Have you ever heard someone talking about a meme you're out of the loop on?
Have you wanted to learn more about this meme, but were you also too lazy to just google the damn thing?
Worry no more, because Node Your Meme now exists!

What is node your meme?

  • A node module that scrapes knowyourmeme
  • An alexa skill that scrapes knowyourmeme
Yeah, that's pretty much it. It can search by term, or give you a random meme on command.
